About Me

Hey, I'm Will Brall. Currently, I am the Lead Web Developer at ZeniMax Online Studios, a published author, and a well-regarded Dungeon Master (at least by my players). Code I wrote has been used by tens of millions of users. Words I've written have been read by hundreds of people. Games I've GMed have been played by dozens of players.

My Web Development and UX Design career has seen me building experiences for The Elder Scrolls Online, the cancelled Commander Keen Mobile game, and the U.S. Army's flagship website. I even designed a game that was in their iOS App. And I started out making intranet sites for NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center.

I wrote Magitism: The Force of Magic in 2022, a book about magical aliens crash-landing on Earth. Dale Arnold, the president of the Baltimore Science Fiction Society, said: "...I enjoyed reading this book more than I have enjoyed Hugo winners in the past."

As a fan of TTRPGs, I've been lucky enough to hobnob with a bunch of D&D legends like Jeff Grubb, Lawrence Schick, Ed Stark, and Zeb Cook, as well as many other giants of the video game industry. Their influence has helped shape me into a world-class Game Master who has run games for many game designers.
